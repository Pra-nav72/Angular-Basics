**** ***** DIRECTIVES ***** ****

In Angular, directives are a core concept—they let you extend or manipulate the DOM (HTML) by attaching behavior to elements.

you need to import commonModule to use directives

Types of Directives

  1. component directives: (@component) --→ directives with templates
  2. structural directives: (*ngIf, *ngFor, *ngSwitch) --→ change layout (add/remove elements)
  3. Attribute directives: ([ngClass], [ngStyle]) --→ change style/behavior

you can also create your own Custom Directive
